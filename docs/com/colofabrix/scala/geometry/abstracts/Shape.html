trait Shape extends Renderable

Represents a geometric closed shape on a geometric space

This train contains definitions of basic geometric operations like distance from a point or a check to see if a line segment intersects the Shape. Upon this elementary operation the derived shapes will build their behaviour and specialize the implementation to take advantage of their properties for a faster access
